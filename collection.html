The collection

The biggest area of the museum is dedicated to paintings.
Here you can find amazing works of art by Tamara de Lempicka, Frida Kahlo, Georgia o´Keefe and many other artists.

The XX century shows a increasing number of female artists that radically transform the art industry and bring innovations.

Not only visual art, but also fashion. Female stylists such as Coco Chanel showed the world that women can be great entrepreneurs as well.

The photographic area tells the history of female art in modern times. You will find Marina Abramovic and many others.

Female art is not only a prerogative of modern times. We have great artists also from other centuries, such as Artemisia Gentileschi.

Don't miss the chance to visit the greatest collection in the world about women in art.
